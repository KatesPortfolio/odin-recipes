How to Make Strawberry Ice Cream

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

    2 cups mashed ripe fresh strawberries
    2 cups whole milk
    2 cups heavy cream
    1 cup white sugar
    2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    Â¼ teaspoon salt
    2 drops red food coloring (Optional)

Instructions

Step 1

Gather all ingredients

Step 2

Combine mashed strawberries, milk, cream, sugar, vanilla, salt, and food coloring in a large bowl

Step 3

Pour strawberry mixture into the freezer bowl of an ice cream maker; freeze according to the manufacturer's directions.

Step 4

Transfer ice cream to an airtight container and freeze until firm.
